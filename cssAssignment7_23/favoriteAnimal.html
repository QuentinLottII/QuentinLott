Pangolin

A Pangolin, or "Scaly Anteater", is a toothless, scale covered mammial native to Asia and Africa. It's an insectivore and is currently endagered, as the animal is overhunted for it's meat in Asi where it's considered a delicay in China and Vietnam. It's my favorite animal as I find it interesting that it's a mammal covered entirely in scales.

There are multiple species of Pangolins in the world. Most of them live in Asia and Africa. All but two are heavily endangered. Those other two are close to being endangered.
